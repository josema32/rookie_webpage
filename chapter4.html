Information Resources on the Web

I must admit that the more I research information on the Internet, the more dependent of it I become. I look for a lot of info in the web. I personally use Google and YouTube the most. I look for information that helps me obtain at least an idea on how to start a new project. It could be building new stairs or remodeling a bathroom, even how to learn a programming language.

From this chapter I can tell that getting current news is one of my likes. I have been able to get them all the time from the Internet. I barely watch them on television. I also like to get the weather forecast from the web. I mostly do it with the Weather Channel app. I have also been a user of Yahoo, MailOnline and MSN in terms of breaking news and celebrity news.

One thing I don’t really do, and is stated on this chapter, is look for people on the web. I’m mostly interested in educational information and how-to information. I have never been attracted or I think needed to look for people. In particular probably about a famous personality I believe but not really targeting someone I know.

In terms of services on the web, the one I use all the time is Amazon. I have occasionally used Craigslist and eBay but I find them less reliable. I think the appearance of their sites have a lot to tell.  

The use of the web for educational information on my end I have always used Wikipedia but looking at the chapter I think that I’ll start using some other resources such as library web sites and encyclopedia web sites.
